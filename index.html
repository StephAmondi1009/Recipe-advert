Dreamy Chevrolet Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 packets of Spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Optional: chili flakes, parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in salted water until all dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© 30 seconds
  3. Pour in cream and simmer 3-4 minutes.
  4. Stir in Parmesan until smooth.
  5. Add pasta + splash of pasta water to loosen sauce.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and chilli flakes.
  7. Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesian.
